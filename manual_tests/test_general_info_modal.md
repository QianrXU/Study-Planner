### Manual Test on the General Info Modal
To test if the general info modal works well, we need to follow the steps below:
#### 1. Run the project with the instructions on [README.md](https://github.com/QianrXU/Study-Planner#how-to-run-the-project)

#### 2. Click 'Create Study Plan'.

![Screen Shot 2022-09-22 at 2 32 49 pm](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/83133588/191674923-4c868622-d9ef-4b6e-b525-22478b81751b.png)

#### 3. Select the course option and starting semester and click 'Continue'.

![Screen Shot 2022-09-22 at 2 33 56 pm](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/83133588/191675135-d951e4cc-b3d5-4584-9027-98ef76178626.png)

#### 4. We should be entering a page that has a column of units on the left and grid on the right.

![Screen Shot 2022-09-22 at 2 36 10 pm](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/83133588/191675367-ce1ecdb0-8ecc-4dd2-ae69-72e5b59ba2b7.png)

#### 5. Scroll down to the bottom of the unit column and locate the large info icon.

![Screen Shot 2022-09-22 at 2 37 11 pm](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/83133588/191675540-1e8d75dc-5433-434b-9608-0dfea82d535a.png)

#### 6. Click the icon and a modal will pop up.

![Screen Shot 2022-09-22 at 2 38 12 pm](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/83133588/191675677-6738a892-c936-4eb8-9ed9-f354590e454e.png)
